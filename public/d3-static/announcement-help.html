PUBLISH - ANNOUNCEMENT FORM HELP

Announcement - Announcements that already exist. Pick "HERE ARE THE AVAILABLE ANNOUNCEMENTS" to create a new one
Name - Name of the Announcement
Campaign - The Campaign the Announcement is associated with
Start Date - The Start Date for the tile to display
End Date - The End Date for the tile to display
Tile Type - The types of tiles available
Tag Line - The top line displayed on the tile
Title - The middle line displayed on the tile
Subtitle - The bottom line displayed on the tile
Icon or Percent - On a 3x1(Icon), it's a link to an image you want to appear on the Tile. On a 3x1(Pie), the percentage of the Pie Chart you want to shade
Color - Hex color of the tile
Published - Is this tile published?
Organization - Check the areas you want to see this announcement. Checking a level makes it available to all levels below it as well
Screens - Which screens do you want this tile to appear on?
